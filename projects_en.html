Projects

Screening Project for the Adus NGO

Objective: The screening project was developed for the Adus NGO with the aim of addressing long queues and assisting refugees in vulnerable situations. The initiative aimed to optimize the screening process to ensure that refugees received assistance more efficiently and quickly.

Screening Project

The project involved creating an automated screening system that significantly reduced wait times and improved the quality of assistance provided to refugees. We implemented solutions to manage and process demands efficiently, using cutting-edge technologies to ensure resources were allocated effectively.

Impact: Through the implementation of this system, we were able to reduce waiting times, allowing the NGO to assist a greater number of refugees in less time. Additionally, the project helped enhance user experience and operational efficiency for the NGO.

Team Creation and Coordination

To ensure the success of these projects, I assembled a diverse team of technology specialists, including developers, data analysts, and infrastructure experts. Each team member contributed their specific expertise, allowing a multifaceted approach to solving the challenges faced by the NGOs.
